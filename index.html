Quick Answers

Things you're
probably wondering.

A few common questions — answered plainly.

Do you work with small budgets?

Yes. Both our current clients started with modest ad budgets. We focus on efficiency first — getting your cost-per-lead down before recommending any scale-up. Small budget doesn't mean small results.

Are you an agency or a freelancer?

Neither, exactly. We're a performance marketing studio — small by design, focused by choice. You work with Sreevin directly, not an account manager. Think of it as agency-level thinking with founder-level accountability.

Do you work outside India?

Currently India-based and primarily serving Indian brands. If you're outside India but targeting the Indian market, we're open to the conversation. Remote-first means geography is rarely a blocker.

How do we get started?

One free 30-minute strategy call. No pitch deck, no obligation, no pressure. Just a conversation about your brand, your goals, and whether we're the right fit for each other. Fill the form below and we'll be in touch within 24 hours.
